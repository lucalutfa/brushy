Meet Brushy.

The AI-powered smart toothbrush that personalizes your oral hygiene and brings the future of dental care to your bathroom.

Discover Brushy

Advanced Oral Care, Simplified.

AI-Powered Analysis

Brushy uses advanced AI to analyze your brushing habits and oral health, providing real-time feedback and personalized recommendations.

Early Issue Detection

Identifies potential dental issues like cavities or gum inflammation at their earliest stages, helping you maintain a healthier smile.

Gamified Experience

Makes brushing fun and engaging with interactive challenges and rewards, motivating you to achieve your oral hygiene goals.

Eco-Friendly Design

Crafted with sustainable materials and designed for durability, Brushy is as kind to the planet as it is to your teeth.

Sleek Design, Smart Technology.

Brushy combines cutting-edge sensor technology with a minimalist, ergonomic design. Its smart sensors map your mouth, ensuring comprehensive coverage and optimal cleaning pressure. All data syncs seamlessly with the Brushy app, providing you with detailed insights and progress tracking.

Inspired by the precision of modern robotics and the elegance of minimalist aesthetics, Brushy is more than a toothbrush – it’s your personal dental hygienist.
